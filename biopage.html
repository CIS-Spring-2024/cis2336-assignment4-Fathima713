The Untold Story

Fathima Afridi

Fathima Afridi is the proud owner of Hexa Cafeteria, located at the University of Houston Sugar Land, and has been working in the business for over 15 years. Afridi has always had a passion towards providding good food and giving everyone around her an enjoyable experience. She values her food and her customers. Afridi started her passion through her mom, who first taught her how to create these special and tasty meals. With time through her mothers mentorship and guidance, she was able to master her skills and obtain a handful of useful experience. Soon with enough experience and her passion, she was able to open her first place "Hexa Cafeteria" which gained it's recongnition for serving various kinds of delicious food. Over there she excelled at providing the best service her customers could have experienced, and she soon gained a lot of popularity for her hard work and dedication.

When she is not working in the kitchen or managing her time, Fathima likes to go on long nature walks and read books under the shadow of a tree. She also likes traveling to different places and learning about different people and their customs and culture. She seems to be obsessed with learning new languages as well. With that, she likes learning different kinds of cusine from different kinds of people.
